Our activity

We are a mobile Pie Mash van with a traditional and modern concept, we sell the traditional pie mash & liquor as well as a range of other pies and gravy.

s well as our regular village weekly locations, as a street food mobile catering can, we also cater for;

  • Private Events
  • Weddings
  • Parties
  • Corporate Events
  • Markets/Festivals
  • Birthdays/Social Events

Aswell as pie mash we also offer an alternative incredible tasting food for evenings at weddings as well as offering mini pies to suit the clients requirements.
